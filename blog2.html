SOCIAL MEDIA

25th november

The pace at which technology is evolving is ever increasing – and people are eager to embrace it. Consider this – in 2019 there were 5.112 bn mobile users, 4.388 bn internet users and 3.484 bn active social media users. All of these groups have grown by between 2 and 9% since 2018 and are expected to continue to grow throughout 2020
